🌴 Top Things to Do Beyond the Resort

  • Nassau Straw Market – shop handmade crafts and souvenirs downtown
  • Clifton Heritage National Park – explore history, hiking trails, and underwater sculptures
  • Rose Island Day Trip (Sandy Toes) – enjoy snorkeling, private beaches, and island vibes
  • Queen’s Staircase & Fort Fincastle – climb the 66 steps and take in the views
  • John Watling’s Distillery – tour a historic rum estate and enjoy free tastings

📶 Stay Connected

Install an eSIM from Airalo to stay connected and avoid roaming charges.

🚗 Getting Around

  • Taxis are easy to find—be sure to agree on the fare before departing.
  • Jitneys (minibuses) are cheap but limited in route and schedule.
  • Want more flexibility? Book a rental car (note: they drive on the left in the Bahamas).

🍽 Where to Eat

  • Arawak Cay Fish Fry – casual, authentic Bahamian seafood
  • Café Matisse – elegant Italian in a cozy garden setting
  • The Poop Deck – seafood and steak with waterfront views
  • Oh Andros! – beloved by locals for fresh grilled fish and hearty portions
  • Wild Thyme – upscale yet relaxed atmosphere with great brunch
